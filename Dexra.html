Our Product: Weez.AI

Weez.AI is an AI-powered teammate that transforms how teams and agencies interact with their digital assets. It allows users to search, summarize, and ask questions about files, images, and videos across platforms.

By combining asset intelligence with creative augmentation, Weez.AI saves time and enables teams to focus on high-value work while the AI handles repetitive tasks.

  • AI Chat for Assets: Interact with your files, images, and videos.
  • Content Automation: Generate presentations, RFPs, and social media creatives.
  • Deep Research: Analyze markets, competitors, and content trends intelligently.
  • Creative Mode: Strategize and plan campaigns with AI assistance.
